# Design Language

Name: **Monochrome Editorial**.

## Principles

1. Express through typography, spacing, composition, assets, and motion—not excessive color.
2. Keep 80% neutral surfaces, 15% tonal contrast, and approximately 5% accent.
3. Use one idea per section and strong whitespace.
4. Detail documentation is calmer than landing/index pages.
5. Dark mode is designed independently, not inverted.

## Typography

Use no more than two font families and four weights. Body text is 16–18px with 1.5–1.65 line height and 60–75 characters per line. Large editorial headings may use tight tracking; body text may not.

## Surfaces

Use semantic variables: page, primary, secondary, elevated, inverse. Avoid rainbow card grids, heavy shadows, excessive glass, and gradient text.
