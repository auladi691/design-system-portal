# Motion DNA

Motion must reveal, explain, or confirm.

| Level | Use | Duration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Micro | hover, copy, tabs | 80–160ms |
| Component | menu, drawer, accordion | 160–240ms |
| Page | filtering, section reveal | 240–360ms |
| Expression | Home hero/showcase only | 400–800ms |

Use small distances (2–24px), ease-out for entering, ease-in for leaving. Avoid scroll hijacking, infinite decorative movement, bounce-heavy springs, or animating every paragraph.

`prefers-reduced-motion` disables parallax, looping decoration, and reveal; functional feedback remains immediate.
